- Tambô bounces beside her, musical notes floating from its body.
- Speech bubble LIS: "Olha quanta gente junto!"
- Speech bubble TAMBÔ: "Isso se chama convivência!"

Panel 2 (top right):
- Scene: close-up of different types of community gatherings shown in three smaller bubbles floating above Lis and Tambô.
- Bubble 1: children playing in a school yard.
- Bubble 2: families at a neighborhood party with music and dancing.
- Bubble 3: people working together building something (community effort).
- Lis points at each bubble curiously, Tambô gestures toward them.
- Speech bubble LIS: "Convivência é estar com pessoas?"
- Speech bubble TAMBÔ: "Sim! E com respeito!"
- Details: each bubble has a warm glow, diverse people in each scene.

Panel 3 (bottom left):

- Lis stands in front of the mural looking at it in wonder, head tilted back.

- Speech bubble TAMBÔ: "O Maracatu é convivência e cultura!"

- Details: the mural is bright and expressive, folk art style, purple and gold dominate.

Panel 4 (bottom right):
- Cozy scene: Lis and Tambô sit in the plaza watching a group of neighbors help an elderly woman carry her groceries.
- Other children wave hello to someone. A shopkeeper gives change with a smile.
