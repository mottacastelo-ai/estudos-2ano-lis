**PROMPT:**
```

Characters: Lis (7-year-old Brazilian girl, long wavy chestnut hair, purple star t-shirt, jeans,
white sneakers) and Foca (vintage antique camera character, sepia-gold body, chrome lens-eye,
purple bow tie, short stumpy legs, friendly face, waist-high next to Lis).

Panel 1 (top left):
- Outdoor scene: bright sunny street in a small Brazilian city, colorful buildings, afternoon light.
- Lis stands in the center of the street looking around with wide curious eyes and a slightly puzzled expression, one hand on chin.
- Foca stands beside her, lens-eye wide open with excitement, one tiny arm pointing forward.
- Background: cheerful storefronts, trees, people walking.
- Speech bubble LIS: "Será que esse lugar sempre foi assim?"
- Speech bubble FOCA: "Boa pergunta, Lis!"
- Details: bright blue sky with small clouds, purple flowers on a nearby tree.

- Close-up scene: Foca holds up a large old sepia-toned photograph with both arms, showing it proudly to Lis.
- The photograph shows an old cobblestone street (paralelepípedos) with horse-drawn carriages and vintage buildings.
- Lis leans forward with eyes wide in surprise, mouth open in an "O".
- Speech bubble FOCA: "Olha como era antes!"
- Speech bubble LIS: "Que diferente!"
- Details: faded golden frame on the photograph, warm sepia tones inside the photo contrasting with the colorful present-day scene.

- Split scene showing two versions of the same street: LEFT half in sepia/vintage style (cobblestones, old buildings, no cars), RIGHT half in bright modern style (asphalt, modern buildings, cars).

- Foca floats slightly above, gesturing with both arms toward both halves.

Panel 4 (bottom right):
- Indoor scene: cozy room with warm lighting, Lis sits cross-legged on the floor.
- In front of her is an open photo album with old sepia photographs on its pages.
- Foca sits next to her, pointing at a photo with a tiny arm, expression soft and warm.

- Speech bubble LIS: "As fotos guardam memórias!"

**PROMPT:**
```

colorful flat illustration, bold black outlines, warm purples and gold tones.
Character reference: same characters —
Lis: long wavy chestnut hair, purple star t-shirt, jeans, white sneakers, big brown eyes.
Foca: sepia-gold vintage camera body, big lens eye, purple bow tie, waist-high, stumpy legs.

Panel 1 (top left):
- Foca projects a holographic image from its lens — a glowing rectangular portal floating in the air.
- LEFT side of portal: sepia-toned 1930s street with few small houses, people on foot, one bicycle, clotheslines between buildings.
- RIGHT side of portal: bright colorful 2004 street with many market stalls, crowds of people shopping, colorful awnings.
- Lis stands in front of the portal, jaw dropped in amazement, pointing at both sides.
- Speech bubble FOCA: "Essa rua em 1930 e em 2004!"
- Speech bubble LIS: "Mudou tanto!"
- Details: glowing purple light around the portal edges, floating in mid-air effect.

Panel 2 (top right):
- Scene: Lis on a very wide modern avenue (Avenida Paulista style) with tall glass buildings and cars.
- Foca holds up a vintage photograph showing the same place as a quiet road with large old houses and trees, pé-direito alto visible.
- Lis compares the real avenue with the photograph, expression of amazement and curiosity.
- Speech bubble LIS: "Antes tinham casas grandes aqui?"

Panel 4 (bottom right):
