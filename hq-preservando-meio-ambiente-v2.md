Cole o bloco de cada página diretamente em:

### PÁGINA 1 — "A floresta de Eciona está em perigo!"
**Objetivo pedagógico:** Apresentar o conceito de ambiente como lar dos seres vivos e mostrar que as ações humanas podem ameaçar esse equilíbrio.

**PROMPT:**
```
Brazilian children's educational comic book page, 4 panels in 2x2 grid,
colorful flat illustration, bold black outlines,
vibrant greens transitioning to heavy grays and browns,
Portuguese speech bubbles, thick panel borders, white gutters.
Mood: starts warm and beautiful, becomes urgent and worried.

Panel 1 (top left):
Beautiful lush forest scene bathed in golden-green light.
The forest is alive: tall trees with full leafy canopies, a clear sparkling stream,
colorful birds on branches, deer in the background, butterflies everywhere.
LIS (green superhero cape with leaf patterns and recycling symbol, wavy chestnut hair,
blue shorts, white sneakers) walks through this forest with arms spread wide,
breathing in deeply, eyes closed in joy.
ECIONA (tiny fairy, leaf wings, mossy-green hair, petal dress, glowing seed wand)
floats beside Lis's shoulder, spreading her arms to show off the forest.
Speech bubble ECIONA (proudly): "Esta é minha floresta, Lis!"
Speech bubble LIS (eyes still closed, smiling): "Que lugar lindo!"

Panel 2 (top right):
ECIONA suddenly freezes in mid-air, her wings drooping, face filled with horror.
She points toward the distance. LIS spins around to follow her gaze.
In the background — visible through the trees — dark smoke rises.
The sound effect "VRRRR" suggests a chainsaw in the distance.
A large tree in the mid-distance slowly tilts and FALLS with a "CRASH!" effect.
Birds fly away in panic from the falling tree. Animals scatter.
LIS's expression: shocked, mouth wide open, hands flying up.
ECIONA's expression: devastated, wings drooping completely, wand tip dimming.
Speech bubble ECIONA (wings drooping): "Não! Estão derrubando as árvores!"
Speech bubble LIS: "Desmatamento!"

Panel 3 (bottom left):
Split "before and after" panel showing two versions of the same forest area:
LEFT SIDE (BEFORE — bright and full of color): lush green forest with animals,
a deer and her fawn, colorful birds on branches, clear stream with fish.
Label (green rounded box): "ANTES: lar de muitos seres vivos"
RIGHT SIDE (AFTER — gray and barren): the same area now shows only tree stumps,
dry cracked earth, no animals, a muddy brown stream, gray sky.
Label (dark rounded box): "DEPOIS: animais perdem o lar"
ECIONA floats on the dividing line between before and after, crying tiny green tears.
Speech bubble ECIONA: "Sem floresta, onde os animais vão viver?"

Panel 4 (bottom right):
LIS kneels down so she is at eye level with the tiny fairy ECIONA.
She holds out her cupped hands and ECIONA lands in them gently.
LIS's expression: determined, eyebrows set, eyes fierce with resolve.
ECIONA looks up at Lis with fragile hope, wings trembling.
Behind them, the threatened forest looms.
Speech bubble LIS (resolute): "Não vamos deixar isso acontecer, Eciona!"
Speech bubble ECIONA (small, hopeful): "Você vai me ajudar?"
Speech bubble LIS: "Sim! Vamos aprender a defender o ambiente!"

Style: colorful Brazilian children's comic, flat design, lush forest to destruction contrast,
before/after split panel, urgent emotional mood shift, fairy in cupped hands moment,
rich greens to heavy grays, bold outlines, Portuguese speech bubbles,
2x2 grid, thick borders, white gutters.
```

## DICAS PARA MELHORES RESULTADOS

- **Eciona** é o personagem mais difícil de gerar por ser muito pequena com detalhes delicados — as asas em formato de folha com nervuras visíveis e a varinha com semente glowing são os detalhes mais críticos. Se as asas saírem como "asas de borboleta" comuns, adicione ao prompt: "wings that look like two large rounded green leaves with visible leaf veins, not butterfly wings."
- **Página 2** é deliberadamente mais escura em tom e paleta — isso é intencional para criar contraste com as páginas de ação positiva. Não ajuste a paleta para ficar mais alegre.
- **Página 1, Painel 3** (before/after split) e **Página 4, Painel 1** (aerial split) são os painéis de maior impacto visual — se não ficarem contrastantes o suficiente, gere-os separadamente.
- Para a **Lis com a capa**, adicione ao prompt: "green superhero cape with leaf patterns printed on it, recycling symbol (♻️) emblem on chest, cape flowing dramatically" — sem isso a capa pode sair como roupa comum.
- Para consistência entre páginas: adicione ao início de cada novo prompt: "Character reference: same characters as previous page — Lis (7-year-old girl, wavy chestnut hair, green superhero cape with leaf patterns and recycling symbol, blue shorts, white sneakers), Eciona (tiny forest fairy, large rounded green leaf-wings with visible veins, mossy-green hair, petal dress, slender wand with glowing green seed tip)."
- Se o texto dos balões sair em inglês, corrija no **Canva**.
- **DALL·E 3** funciona melhor para cenas ambientais (Páginas 1, 3, 4). **Ideogram** funciona melhor para painéis de diagrama/comparação (Página 2 Painel 4, Página 4 Painel 2).
