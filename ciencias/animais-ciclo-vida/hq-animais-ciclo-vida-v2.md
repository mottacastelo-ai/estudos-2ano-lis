### PÁGINA 1 — "Lis encontra Tarta no jardim"
**Objetivo pedagógico:** Introduzir o conceito de ciclo de vida e despertar a curiosidade da criança sobre como os animais nascem e crescem.

**PROMPT:**
```
Brazilian children's educational comic book page, 4 panels in 2x2 grid,
colorful flat illustration, bold black outlines, vibrant greens and warm yellows,
Portuguese speech bubbles, thick panel borders, white gutters.
Warm outdoor nature setting, wonder-filled garden mood, golden afternoon light.

Panel 1 (top left):
Lush garden scene with green grass, wildflowers, and a leafy tree in the background.
LIS (7-year-old girl, wavy chestnut hair to shoulders, green star t-shirt, blue shorts, white sneakers)
crouches low on the grass, holding a large magnifying glass up to a small nest on the ground.
Inside the nest: four round greenish-white eggs nestled in straw.
Lis's expression: eyes opened very wide in pure wonder, mouth forming a delighted "O".
A yellow butterfly flutters past the upper right corner of the panel.
Dappled sunlight through tree branches creates soft shadows on the grass.
Speech bubble LIS (pointing at eggs with free hand): "Que ovinhos pequeninhos!"

Panel 2 (top right):
Close-up on ONE egg from the nest — it is now cracking open.
Multiple fine zigzag cracks spread across the shell from a central point.
A tiny turtle head pushes through the crack: it is baby TARTA, impossibly small and adorable,
eyes huge and blinking, shell still soft-looking with a brighter mossy green color.
LIS kneels at the edge of the panel, both hands pressing her cheeks, eyes shining.
A warm golden glow radiates outward from the cracking egg like a tiny sunrise.
Speech bubble LIS: "Está nascendo! Que incrível!"
Caption box (rounded green at top of panel): "Todo animal tem um começo..."

Panel 3 (bottom left):
TARTA is now fully adult — same height as Lis's torso, wire-frame glasses perfectly placed, warm smile.
She stands upright on the grass facing LIS, the cracked empty eggshell sitting between them.
LIS stares with wide confused eyes and a tilted head, one finger pointing at Tarta.
A large glowing orange question mark floats above Lis's head.
The garden behind them is sunnier and brighter than before.
Speech bubble TARTA (pushing glasses up her nose with one stubby leg): "Olá, Lis! Sou eu, Tarta!"
Speech bubble LIS: "Mas... você cresceu tão rápido?!"

Panel 4 (bottom right):
TARTA laughs with delight, her shell shaking slightly with the laugh, eyes crinkled.
She holds up a small hand-drawn scroll with a simple illustrated cycle drawn on it:
EGG → tiny baby turtle → larger young turtle → adult turtle, connected by curved arrows.
LIS leans forward eagerly with clasped hands, standing on tiptoe, face bright with curiosity.
Soft glowing fireflies begin to appear around them as the afternoon turns golden.
Fallen leaves drift gently around their feet.
Speech bubble TARTA: "Vou te contar sobre o ciclo de vida!"
Speech bubble LIS: "Ciclo de vida? O que é isso?"

Style: colorful Brazilian children's comic, flat design, lush garden setting,
hatching egg wonder sequence, wise turtle character introduction,
golden afternoon nature lighting, bold outlines, Portuguese speech bubbles,
2x2 grid, thick borders, white gutters.
```

## DICAS PARA MELHORES RESULTADOS

- **Tarta** é o personagem mais difícil de manter consistente — os óculos de arame redondos e o padrão hexagonal do casco são os detalhes-chave. Regenere a folha de personagens até ter uma versão sólida antes de gerar as páginas.
- **Página 2, Painel 2** (diagrama dividido ovíparos vs vivíparos) é o painel mais complexo — se os dois lados não ficarem equilibrados, gere-o separadamente com um prompt focado exclusivamente nesse painel.
- **Página 3, Painel 1** (timeline das 4 fases do cachorro) também pode precisar de geração separada para que os 4 estágios fiquem distintos e legíveis.
- Para consistência entre páginas: adicione ao início de cada novo prompt: "Character reference: same characters as previous page — Lis (7-year-old girl, wavy chestnut hair to shoulders, green star t-shirt, light blue shorts, white sneakers with green laces), Tarta (wise turtle, dark green hexagonal shell, small round wire-frame glasses, yellowish-green wrinkled skin, reaches Lis's chest height)."
- Se o texto dos balões sair em inglês, corrija no **Canva** adicionando caixas de texto sobre as imagens.
- **DALL·E 3** funciona melhor para cenas de natureza (Páginas 1 e 4). **Ideogram** funciona melhor para painéis de diagrama (Páginas 2 e 3).
