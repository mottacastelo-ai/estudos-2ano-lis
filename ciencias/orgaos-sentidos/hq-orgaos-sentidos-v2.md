### PÁGINA 1 — "Sensinho aparece — cinco sentidos para explorar!"
**Objetivo pedagógico:** Apresentar os cinco sentidos e seus respectivos órgãos como ferramentas que usamos para perceber o mundo ao redor.

**PROMPT:**
```
Brazilian children's educational comic book page, 4 panels in 2x2 grid,
colorful flat illustration, bold black outlines, vibrant greens and metallic blues,
Portuguese speech bubbles, thick panel borders, white gutters.
Energetic introduction mood — sense of wonder and discovery, playful robotic sparkle.

Panel 1 (top left):
LIS (7-year-old girl, wavy chestnut hair, green star t-shirt, blue shorts, white sneakers)
walks through a colorful garden full of flowers, butterflies, and a distant bird singing.
She has her eyes closed and arms slightly spread, head tilted as if listening.
Her expression: peaceful concentration, eyebrows gently raised.
Colorful swirls represent invisible sensations around her:
golden sparkles near her eyes, blue sound waves near her ears, pink wisps near her nose.
Speech bubble LIS (eyes still closed): "O mundo tem tantas coisas para sentir..."

Panel 2 (top right):
SENSINHO suddenly pops into view — appearing from behind a large flower with a cheerful "POP!" sound effect.
His five antennas (golden eye, blue ear, pink nose, orange tongue, teal hand) are all lit up and sparkling.
His chest screen glows with a bright "?" symbol.
LIS spins around in surprise, hair flying slightly, mouth forming a big O.
Speech bubble SENSINHO (all antennas sparkling): "Olá, Lis! Sou o Sensinho!"
Speech bubble LIS: "Ahhh! Quem é você?!"

Panel 3 (bottom left):
SENSINHO hovers at eye level with LIS, showing off his five antennas proudly.
Each antenna tip glows brightly in its own color.
He uses one hand to point to his antennas one by one.
LIS leans in close, eyes wide with fascination, hands behind her back.
The background shows simple illustrated icons around them: a sun, a bell, a flower, a strawberry, a feather.
Speech bubble SENSINHO (pointing to each antenna): "Cada antena é um dos seus 5 sentidos!"
Speech bubble LIS: "Cinco sentidos? Só conheço dois ou três!"

Panel 4 (bottom right):
Large illustrated diagram panel — SENSINHO hovers beside a simple drawing of a human face/body outline.
Five colored arrows point from each sense organ to its label:
Arrow from eye (golden): "👁️ VISÃO — olhos"
Arrow from ear (blue): "👂 AUDIÇÃO — ouvidos"
Arrow from nose (pink): "👃 OLFATO — nariz"
Arrow from mouth/tongue (orange): "👅 PALADAR — língua"
Arrow from skin/hand (teal): "🖐️ TATO — pele"
LIS studies the diagram intently, one finger on her chin, thoroughly impressed.
SENSINHO's chest screen now shows a checkmark.
Speech bubble SENSINHO: "Juntos, eles mostram o mundo!"
Speech bubble LIS: "Nunca pensei nisso assim!"

Style: colorful Brazilian children's comic, flat design, garden introduction scene,
robot surprise appearance, five-antenna reveal, labeled body diagram,
vibrant greens and metallic blues, bold outlines, Portuguese speech bubbles,
2x2 grid, thick borders, white gutters.
```

## DICAS PARA MELHORES RESULTADOS

- **Sensinho** é o personagem mais difícil de manter consistente — as 5 antenas coloridas com ícones específicos na ponta são o detalhe visual mais importante. Regenere a folha de personagens várias vezes se necessário.
- **A cara de azedo de Lis** (Página 3, Painel 3) é uma das cenas de maior impacto da HQ — se não sair cômica o suficiente, gere esse painel separadamente.
- **Página 4, Painel 2** (o diagrama da flor dos 5 sentidos) é o painel mais complexo — gere-o separadamente se precisar.
- Para consistência entre páginas: adicione ao início de cada novo prompt: "Character reference: same characters as previous page — Lis (7-year-old girl, wavy chestnut hair, green star t-shirt, blue shorts, white sneakers), Sensinho (small round metallic robot, light-blue body, five colored antennas with sense organ tips: golden eye, blue ear, pink nose, orange tongue, teal hand)."
- Se o texto dos balões sair em inglês, corrija no **Canva** adicionando caixas de texto sobre as imagens.
- **DALL·E 3** funciona melhor para cenas de ambiente (jardim, cozinha, festa). **Ideogram** funciona melhor para os painéis de diagrama (Páginas 1 Painel 4, Página 4 Painel 2).
- A consistência das CORES das antenas é crítica para o impacto visual — certifique-se de que cada antena mantenha sua cor em todas as páginas.
